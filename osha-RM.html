Occupational Safety and Health Risk Management Certificate

Training Course Outline

This is Occupational Safety and Health Risk Management course offered by FACTORIES AND GENERAL LABOUR LAW INSPECTIONS DEPARTMENT .

Course outlines are as follows:

  • Basic concept of occupational safety and health
  • Identifying occupational hazards
  • Calculation and evaluation of workplace risk
  • The concept of controlling workplace hazards
  • Workplace risk management
